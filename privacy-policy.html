Privacy Policy — TikTokAutoPost

Last updated: June 30, 2026

TikTokAutoPost ("the App") is a personal tool used to publish video content to the App owner's own TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API.

Information We Collect

  • Basic TikTok account information obtained via TikTok Login (such as username and user ID) after you authorize the App through TikTok's OAuth process.
  • Video files and captions that you choose to upload through the App.

How We Use Information

  • Solely to authenticate with your TikTok account and to publish the video content you provide to that same account.
  • We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing your content to TikTok.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.
  • Data is only transmitted to TikTok's official API to perform the posting action you requested.

Data Storage

Access tokens and video files are stored only as long as needed to perform the publishing action and are not retained beyond that purpose.

Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via your TikTok account settings (Settings > Security > Manage Third-Party Logins).
